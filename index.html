About Me

I'm Front End Web Developer.

I am a Certified Real Estate and Mortgage broker in Europe.
My specialty is Economics and Management. For 2 years now I have been professionally developing in the field of IT technologies, namely as a Web Developer. Continuous training, constant practice will help you in creating a website in order to profitably represent you in business, for a future employer or for your potentional clients. In creating the site, I use HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I can easily work in a team using Github.

In the next 2 years (2021-2022), I plan to gain skills in: JSX syntax, React JS, Angular, Vue JS, API, Node JS, PHP, MySQL. I am also studying as a Junior Accountant (International accounting and taxation). My mission is to be useful to the society, to develop myself and people around me. My goal is to be a qualified specialist, a successful businessman and a reliable partner.
